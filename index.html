Services

End-to-end consulting, free of supplier bias

Four focused services that protect the client's interest — efficient layouts, accurate documentation, optimized pricing, and smooth coordination.

Service 01

Design, Layouts & Presentations

Space planning and furniture layouts for open offices, private offices, meeting rooms, and executive areas — optimized for workflow, budget, and ergonomic & circulation standards.

Deliverables: Layout plans, furniture zoning, seating-capacity analysis, presentation files (PDF / PowerPoint).

Service 02

Bill of Quantities (BOQ)

Detailed BOQs with item descriptions, dimensions, finishes, quantities, and optional alternatives — fully aligned with approved layouts.

Deliverables: Editable Excel BOQ and a PDF BOQ ready for tendering — eliminating errors and missing items.

Service 03

Quotation & Price Comparison

Quotations prepared across American manufacturers, European manufacturers, and local factories, with cost comparison, value engineering, and budget-optimization options.

Deliverables: Comparative pricing tables, a recommendation report, and budget-vs-specification analysis.

Service 04

Client–Manufacturer Coordination

Acting as the technical and commercial bridge — supporting clarifications, specification alignment, and negotiations so client requirements are clearly communicated.

Advisory role only. No furniture trading. Full transparency.
